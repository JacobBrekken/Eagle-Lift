about us

Every day is a chance to become better

Welcome to Eagle Lift! We're your hub for fitness, sports, and wellness on campus, offering top-notch facilities and programs to help you thrive physically and socially during your college journey at UNT.

improved focus

Regular exercise boosts cognitive function, helping students stay alert and focused during classes and study sessions.

stress relief

Gym sessions provide an outlet for releasing tension and stress, promoting better mental well-being and reducing anxiety levels.

enhanced mood

Exercise triggers the release of endorphins, chemicals in the brain that act as natural mood lifters, fostering a positive outlook on academic and personal challenges.

better sleep

Physical activity promotes deeper and more restful sleep, essential for academic performance and overall health.
